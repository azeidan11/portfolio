A bit about me

Hi, I'm Adam Zeidan, a student developer in California with a passion for creating impactful software using a diverse set of tools and technologies. My experience spans across languages like Java, C++, Python, JavaScript, and TypeScript, with a strong foundation in web technologies such as HTML5, CSS3, React, and Next.js. I focus on building responsive, scalable, and visually polished applications using frameworks like Tailwind CSS, Spring Boot, and Node.js, while leveraging modern tools like LangChain, Prisma, and Hugging Face for AI-driven functionality.

Beyond development, I’ve published research on workforce trends in AI in the International Journal of Progressive Research in Science and Engineering. This work analyzed AI’s impact on industries and job satisfaction, offering recommendations for aligning education with evolving market needs. I’m committed to continuous learning and creating solutions that address real-world challenges through collaboration and innovation.
